# 📊 The Digital Skills Paradox in India  
### Access, Capability, and Utilisation Gaps (NSSO 80th Round Analysis)

---

## 📌 Project Overview
India’s digital transformation is often framed as a success story driven by rapid internet expansion, falling data costs, and large-scale policy initiatives. However, this project challenges that narrative by uncovering a critical but underexplored issue:

> **The Digital Skills Paradox — the coexistence of digital capability without meaningful utilisation.**

Using nationally representative NSSO 80th Round Telecom Survey data, this study demonstrates that **having access and skills does not guarantee their actual use**, revealing a deeper layer of digital inequality.

---

## 🎯 Research Question
> In what way do the factors of household access, digital skill and usage context vary and 
contribute to the continuity of the capability-utilisation gap in India, even among active 
internet users? 

---

## 📊 Data Source
- NSSO 80th Round Modular Telecom Survey 
- Nationally representative microdata
- Two datasets:
  - **CMST80HH** → Household-level access & socio-economic data  
  - **CMST80PER** → Individual-level skills, usage, and behaviour :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

## ⚙️ Methodology

### 🔹 Data Preparation
- Merged household (CMST80HH) and person-level (CMST80PER) datasets  
- Integrated socio-economic, access, and usage variables  

---

### 🔹 Key Indices
- **IDCS (Individual Digital Capability Score)** → Measures digital capability  
- **HAQI (Household Access Quality Index)** → Captures quality of access  
- **CUG (Capability–Utilisation Gap)** → Measures unused digital skills  

---

### 🔹 Analytical Strategy

#### 1. Descriptive Analysis
- Comparison of capability (IDCS) and utilisation gaps (CUG) across:
  - Caste, gender, income, and rural–urban sectors  

---

#### 2. Regression Models
- **Logistic Regression** → Binary outcomes (usage, skill non-use)  
- **Linear Regression** → Continuous outcomes (IDCS, CUG)  

---

#### 3. Extended Models
- Multinomial models → Gap severity  
- Interaction models → Device × location effects  
- Robustness checks → Restricted to active internet users  

---

### 🎯 Focus
To explain **why digital capability does not translate into actual usage**, even when access exists.
